# üü¶ **1. Indian Government ‚Äì Water & Environment APIs (data.gov.in)**

**URL:** [https://data.gov.in](https://data.gov.in)
**Type:** Fully open, free, CSV/JSON, official.

### You get data for:

* River water levels
* Reservoir water storage
* Groundwater depth
* Dams information (statewise)
* Rainfall data (real-time + historical)
* Flood forecast
* Hydrology & irrigation datasets
* Water quality of rivers/lakes
* Groundwater contamination (arsenic, fluoride)

### Developer Notes:

* Many datasets have **free API endpoints** with keys
* Pagination & rate limits apply
* Best for **Indian-specific water monitoring dashboards**
* Very stable
